Organisations

Mental health, wellbeing & crisis support for organisations

Mental Health

  • Training (leaders, managers, HR, peers, union officials): Practical, audience-specific training so the right people in your organisation know how to spot difficulty early and respond well.
  • Programmes: Structured, multi-session interventions that go beyond a single training day.
  • Understanding mental health: Awareness-raising that builds genuine understanding of mental health conditions, not just a checklist of symptoms.

Wellbeing

  • Strategy consultation: Helping you think through what a genuine wellbeing strategy looks like for your organisation, beyond a tick-box policy.
  • Audits: A clear-eyed assessment of what's already working, what isn't, and where the gaps are.
  • Programmes: Structured, multi-session wellbeing interventions designed around your people and your context.
  • Guidance: Ongoing, ad hoc support for leaders and wellbeing leads as things come up.

Keynotes & Speaking

  • Conferences / events / away days: Keynotes and talks for conferences, leadership events, and team away days.
  • Sample topics: crisis readiness, mindful leadership, building resilient teams, what a proper wellbeing strategy looks like, how to cope in a crisis.

Crisis & Resilience

  • Organisational readiness: Helping you prepare before a crisis hits, not just react once it has — drawing on a tried and tested framework.
  • Critical incident and post-incident response (including suicide and self-harm scenarios): Direct, experienced support through and after a critical incident.

Retreats & Offsites

  • Team events: Structured days designed to bring a team together away from the day-to-day.
  • Leadership retreats: Reflective space for leadership groups to step back and think clearly.
  • Bespoke retreats: Fully custom-designed to a specific brief, when something off-the-shelf won't fit.

Training

  • Open / standard programmes: Established training programmes covering wellbeing, mindfulness, resilience, and counselling skills etc.
  • CPD for practitioners: Deeper skill development for professionals already qualified in their field.
  • Bespoke / in-house: Training built specifically around your organisation's brief and context.
