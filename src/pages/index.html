About Bruce

Bruce Greenhalgh a certified mindfulness teacher through Bangor University and an SIY teacher for the Search Inside Yourself programme developed at Google.

He has taught mindfulness internationally, was the Senior Wellbeing Specialist in BT and is the Council Director for the Executive Wellbeing Council at The Conference Board. He is also BACP accredited, Fellow of Chartered Institute of Personnel & Development a psychotherapist and a tutor on an Adlerian counselling training course in the UK. Learn more
