About Bruce

A path shaped by one question

Bruce Greenhalgh is a certified mindfulness teacher through Bangor University and an SIY teacher for the Search Inside Yourself programme developed at Google. He has taught mindfulness internationally, was the Senior Wellbeing Specialist at BT, and is Council Director for the Executive Wellbeing Council at The Conference Board.

He is also BACP accredited, a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Personnel & Development, a psychotherapist, and a tutor on an Adlerian counselling training course in the UK.
